Events at S&L

Almost every week at S&L, you can find events run by our game masters and community managers to suit your need!


From monthly tournaments of the biggest games for those with competitive spirit to introductions to some of the most popular board games, there are events to cater to all levels and interests. All of our events are free to attend ensuring that no-one is left out!


On this page you can find more information about our biggest games, along with a calendar featuring all of the events at S&L over the next five months!


Can't find what you need? Get in touch!

Major Events

Warhammer 40k

Epic tabletop battles in the grim darkness of the 41st millenium. S&L hosts in-house tournaments every other month that are open to all who are interested in the hobby. A perfect place to flex your competitive muscles or learn a bit more about the game!

New to 40k? Find out more at the official Warhammer site!

Magic the Gathering

The world's greatest trading card game! S&L is home to a number of enthusiastic Magic collectors and players, with our monthly tournaments being a hotspot for both local players and those from further afield!

New to Magic the Gathering? Find out more at the official Magic site!

D&D Adventure League

From the bustling cities of the Sword Coast, to the dark and foreboding Gothic castle of Ravenloft, and all the forests, mountains and fields between, Dungeons & Dragons can take you on epic adventures! The S&L Adventure League offers a way for you to bring along a character to many of these adventures on a drop-in basis, and is the perfect place to meet more players for a longer term campaign!

New to D&D? Find out more at the official D&D site!
