What is Shakes & Ladders?

S&L provides a welcoming community space for all to come and enjoy playing board games, whether they be the classic family games we all know and love or the latest in competitive strategy games.

From complete beginners to hardened veterans, we host regular events for players of all levels. Whether you are interested in competing against other players or you want to learn something totally new, there are events held every week at S&L. Find out more!
